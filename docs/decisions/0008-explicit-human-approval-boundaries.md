# ADR 0008: Explicit Human Approval Boundaries

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The product is intended to keep the developer in control without adding friction to every minor action.

## Decision

Require human approval at strong gates: plan approval, scope expansion, destructive commands, integration of specialist work, and final commit.

## Consequences

- Maintains trust and control
- Avoids approval spam for every small edit
- Requires a durable approval model and good pending-approval UX
